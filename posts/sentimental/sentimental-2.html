  • Real or not, you tell me.
  • Not everything started out sour and rotten. As you progress my study in General Biology, you realize that life at a microscopic level is an accurate projection of life played out on a grand scale. Years ago, life was like a piece of wagyu beef that you can only find in an upscale, socialite restaurant. But it was mercilessly discarded and disregarded because of its minor imperfections. Then the piece of wagyu slowly decomposes from a stunning AAA-grade cut with silver-lined fat in between and only a few untrimmed corners to just another garbage that disgusts whomever comes by.
  • Welcome to the story of my life, kids.
  • I rarely hold grudge against people. But there are ones that I just cannot forgive nor forget because of the tremendous and destructive consequences that the particular actions and solutions that they chose fit for enacting on us, more especially me. Were those sanctions necessary? Till this day I still wonder. The ponderation eats away my brain little by little, takes part in ripping the living soul out of my already exhausted corpse.
  • Changing seats, changing class, changing schools, interventions, etc. Did you bastards think that those would do right? You graduated pedagogy but acted like a bunch of mechanical marionettes. I have gratitude for those who supported during what supposed to be the best time of my (our) lives. Yet, you emotionless creatures beautifully marvelously sundered and lysed the one thing that kept me sane from the over-studying for high school entrance exam.
  • I don’t know whether us getting to the top high school in the whole country is a testament to our not being distracted like you motherfuckers thought we did or just a concrete evidence to solidify your animosity towards the relationship. To me, it seems more like the troubles and threats that I got myself into are the results of your overwhelming pedagogic superego.
  • For that, I’d say good job well done. Because of that, you managed to become an important facilitation to my demise. My walk towards the void and the land of endless suffrage pays a tribute to your soullessness.
