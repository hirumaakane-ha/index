What is The Chronicle?

The Chronicle is a narration started by me to simply tell a visualizing story.

Why are you creating a new blog?

This particular narration doesn't really fit the other blogging topics that I have created. Those are more about my experience. This time, with The Chronicle, I'd like to become a different person and see the world through a different set of lenses, completely unbiased by my own experience and stories. You could say that it is a literary rebirth for me.
